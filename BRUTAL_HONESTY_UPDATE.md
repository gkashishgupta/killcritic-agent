## How It Works

### Input Analysis
```
Score calculation:
- Base: 5.0
- Length penalty: (-2 if <50 chars, -0.5 if <100)
- Problem clarity: +0.3 per keyword (problem, pain point, etc.)
- Market understanding: +0.4 per market keyword
- Business model: +0.3 per revenue model keyword
- Validation: +0.5 per validation keyword
- Red flags: -0.5 per flag (hope, maybe, eventually, etc.)
```

### Analysis Output

**What You Get:**
1. **VERDICT** - Pass/Fail/Maybe with honest reasoning
2. **Executive Summary** - Brutal assessment in 2-3 sentences
3. **Clarity Assessment** - Direct feedback on idea articulation
4. **Market Opportunity** - Honest TAM and market reality
5. **Competitive Reality** - True competitive analysis
6. **Score Grid** - 4 different metrics scored
7. **Strengths** - Real advantages (if any exist)
8. **FATAL FLAWS** - Critical issues that could kill this
9. **Major Risks** - Execution, market, competitive, regulatory
10. **What You MUST Do** - Non-negotiable next steps
11. **Build Plan** - 3-phase MVP roadmap
12. **Final Viability Score** - 1-10 rating
13. **Failure Risk** - Probability assessment
